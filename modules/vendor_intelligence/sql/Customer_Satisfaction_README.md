This module evaluates customer experience across delivery partners, regions, and operational conditions by analyzing delivery ratings and their relationship with delays and vendor performance. It enables OpsBrain to identify service quality risks and recommend operational improvements to protect customer satisfaction.

Scope

This module answers questions like:

Which delivery partners have the lowest customer satisfaction?

Do delivery delays reduce customer ratings?

Which regions have poor service quality?

Which vendors perform poorly when deliveries are delayed?

Where should we improve service to protect customer experience?

It does NOT handle:

Vendor cost optimization and cost leakage
(Handled by the Vendor Performance module)

Root cause analysis of operational delays
(Handled by the Delivery Delay module)

Core KPIs

Customer Satisfaction KPIs:

Average Customer Rating per Vendor

Average Customer Rating for Delayed vs On-Time Deliveries

Regional Customer Satisfaction Score

Vendor Rating Under Delay Conditions

Customer Satisfaction Distribution by Delivery Partner
